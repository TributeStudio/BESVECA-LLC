# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Eric and Jessica use this private operating tool to manage BESVECA House guests, stays, charges, invoices, payment schedules, and booking status. They need quick, accurate records during active guest coordination and clean, guest-ready printed invoices.

## Product Purpose

Keep BESVECA House rental billing accurate and operational from guest setup through payment. Success means every invoice clearly communicates stay dates and times, original charges, discounts, fees, payment requirements, and standard property terms without requiring manual reconstruction.

## Brand Personality

Warm, composed, and trustworthy. The product should feel like the operational side of a carefully run Palm Springs hospitality property: polished enough for guests, quiet enough for repeated administrative use.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS decoration, dense accounting jargon, ornamental dashboards, print artifacts, clipped invoice content, dark overlays in printed output, and interfaces that make the user infer whether a booking is confirmed.

## Design Principles

1. Make money and booking status explicit.
2. Preserve a clear audit trail from original price through discounts and final total.
3. Treat guest-facing print output as a primary product surface.
4. Use familiar administrative controls and restrained visual hierarchy.
5. Keep standard BESVECA terms consistent across every invoice.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable type, strong contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, reduced-motion-safe behavior, and print layouts that remain legible in grayscale. Target WCAG 2.1 AA for interactive surfaces.
